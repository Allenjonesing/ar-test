3D + GPS Experiments

3D GPS AR/VR Lab

A suite of experiments mixing real GPS tracking with 3D rendering — from location-anchored AR markers to building an entire VR world from your real walk. Every demo is GPS + 3D.

🌍

GPS World Walker

Two-phase flagship demo. Phase 1: walk around recording your GPS trail on a live map. Phase 2: enter a first-person 3D corridor world generated from your exact GPS path. Your real walk becomes your virtual world.

GPS · Three.js · First-Person
🥽

A-Frame GPS VR World

Record your GPS trail, then immerse yourself in a full VR scene built from it using A-Frame. Glowing pillar markers, corridor floor panels, and a gyroscope-driven look-around — your walk becomes a VR level.

A-Frame · VR · GPS
🌐

A-Frame GPS AR Trail

Location-based AR via A-Frame + AR.js. Coloured 3D sphere markers appear in the live camera view anchored to exact GPS coordinates as you walk. Watch your trail build in augmented reality.

A-Frame · AR.js · GPS
📡

WebXR GPS Anchor AR

Native WebXR AR session with GPS-anchored 3D pillar markers overlaid on the camera feed. Compass + motion-derived heading calibration keeps objects locked to GPS positions. Falls back to a 3D preview if WebXR unavailable.

WebXR · Three.js · GPS
🔭

3D GPS Path Viewer

Three.js 3D bird's-eye visualisation of your GPS trail as you walk. Orbit, zoom, and rotate freely around your path. Real-time GPS updates with waypoint markers — no AR, pure 3D GPS mapping.

Three.js · GPS · 3D Map
