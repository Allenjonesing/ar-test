GPS Journey Live Dashboard

Real-time journey tracker with live speed, distance, max speed, heading compass and a colour-coded path canvas. Speed is painted green → amber → red as you move faster.

Accuracy: ~5–15 m GPS · speed calculated from fixes

GPS · World Gen · Browser

Waypoint Room Builder

Walk a loop and drop up to 12 GPS waypoints. After validation the app builds a dungeon-style corridor map from your real-world path — only where you've already walked. Then step inside a full 3D virtual world of your route and explore it first-person using your phone camera.

Accuracy: ~5–15 m GPS · min 5 m waypoint spacing
Use case: GPS world generation; 3D virtual walkthroughs
Requires: Any mobile browser · HTTPS · Geolocation

GPS Territory Grid

Fill in a GPS grid as you explore — every cell you walk lights up. Speed-coded colours (green → red), visit-count overlays, achievement unlocks, and a coverage donut tracker.
