GPS Trail Lab

Six brand-new approaches to GPS path recording and navigation — no WebXR, no XR coordinate drift. Each experiment uses a completely different toolkit or technique so you can find what actually works for your device.

📍

GPS Canvas Trail

Pure vanilla JS + Canvas 2D. Records your GPS path and draws it in real-time as you walk. No frameworks, no drift — just raw GPS on a canvas. Stores your trail in localStorage and exports GeoJSON.

Vanilla JS
🗺️

Live Map Trail

Your GPS trail drawn on a real OpenStreetMap using Leaflet.js. See exactly where you've been on a proper map. Auto-follows you as you walk. Export your route as GeoJSON.

Leaflet.js
🌐

A-Frame GPS AR

Location-based AR using A-Frame + AR.js. Coloured spheres appear at your previous GPS positions in camera view. Completely different from WebXR — anchored to GPS coordinates directly, no XR tracking system.

A-Frame + AR.js
🌐

3D Path Viewer

Three.js 3D scene (no WebXR) showing your GPS trail from above. Orbit, zoom and rotate freely. Path builds in real-time as you walk. Zero coordinate drift — no AR session involved.

Three.js · No AR
🌍

GPS World Builder

Drop named, emoji-tagged place markers anywhere you go. Your personal world map builds up as you explore. Record a walking trail too. Everything persists in localStorage and exports as GeoJSON.

World Builder
🧭

Compass Navigator

Visual compass dial that points toward saved GPS waypoints. Shows distance and bearing to each target in real-time. Add waypoints manually, from GPS, or import from World Builder. Works completely offline.

GPS Nav
