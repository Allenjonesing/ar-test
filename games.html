🐕 Dog Walk Tips for GPS Games

  • 📱 Keep your phone screen on and brightness high for easy glancing while holding the lead. Most games auto-update in the background.
  • 🌳 GPS accuracy is best in open areas. Urban canyons and dense tree cover can reduce accuracy to 10–20 m, so concepts should prefer larger pickup/chase radii.
  • 🔋 GPS is battery-intensive. Consider a portable charger for long walks. The games only use GPS — no camera or AR hardware required.
  • 🦴 In Fetch Quest, bones are currently placed on nearby roads and pavements. Future concepts should prioritize sidewalk placement where possible.
  • 🧭 For chase modes, a clear heading/facing indicator is critical so players can react quickly when monsters appear behind or beside them.
